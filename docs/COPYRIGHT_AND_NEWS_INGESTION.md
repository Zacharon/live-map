# Copyright and News Ingestion

News and social sources are discovery and corroboration inputs by default.

Rules:

- Store metadata, links, timestamps, publisher names, and short permitted snippets only.
- Do not republish full copyrighted articles unless a license explicitly allows it.
- Preserve canonical source URLs.
- Clearly mark discovery-only sources as discovery, not confirmation.
- Handle corrections, retractions, and takedowns.

GDELT, Google News RSS, Media Cloud, social APIs, and similar sources must not be treated as primary confirmation without independent support.
